Firestone Idle RPG

Firestone Heroes is a casual idle RPG game that can fight automatically or manually manipulate hero skills.

Introduction

Firestone Heroes is a casual idle RPG game that can fight automatically or manually manipulate hero skills. The story takes the world of El continent. The human kingdom is invaded by undead creatures and orcs, creating chaos in the kingdom. While humans are busy dealing with enemies, the claws of the dark forces have reached the castle and stole the treasure of the country “Firestone Heart” ,which hidden deep in the palace tower.

In order to maintain the peace of the world of El continent and regain the “Firestone Heart”, the human kingdom and the elves have formed an alliance and embarked on a journey against the forces of darkness.

Hero

Heroes are warriors of this world. They are divided into fighters, wizards, priests, and druids according to their occupations, and they are divided into output, tanks, and treatments according to their specialization. Each hero has unique combat skills, which can be activated by using hero resources (mana, anger or energy).

Team and formation

Like all classic RPG games, you can gather a team of 5 heroes to fight for you. In the team, you need to deploy the best heroes and professions, because the team’s lineup is the key to victory.

Equipment

The hero’s equipment is as powerful as them! Find the best equipment and put it on the hero. Improve the quality of equipment and enchant to make equipment more powerful.
